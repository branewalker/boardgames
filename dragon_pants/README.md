#Dragon Pants

*Alternatively, "Dragon Trousers" in the U.K. and Europe*

Designed by Megan Bednarz and Lars Doucet

##Details

2-6 players  
Playtime: ~30 minutes

##Object of the game

The dragon who holds The Pants when the game's timer expires wins the game.

##Contents

- 6 dragon pawns
- 43 hexagonal board tiles
  - 27 land tiles
  - 6 dragon den tiles
  - 4 mountain tiles
  - 3 water tiles
  - 3 power tiles
  
 - Deck of Power: 26 cards
   - The Pants x1
   - Fireball x2
   - Shock x2
   - Shield x2
   - Freeze x2
   - Boost x2
   - Poison x1
   - Amulet x1
   - Tongue x1
   - Banish x1
   - Thunderstorm x1
   - Teleport x1
   - Rubber Ducky x1
   - Mirror x1
   - Blank x7
   
 - Deck of Destiny: 26 cards
   - (-1) x13
   - (-2) x3
   - (-3) x1
   - (+1) x3
   - (+2) x2
   - Blank x4
